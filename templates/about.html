The Maker

Monu Verma

I built Carrier Codex after class 10th — sitting with the same confusion most students face when asked to pick a stream without real data, without honest numbers, and without anyone who would tell them what careers actually look like from the inside.

This project started as a data analytics exercise and grew into something I wish had existed when I needed it. Every chart, every dataset, every insight here was researched and structured by hand, notebook by notebook.

What This Project Is

A Data Analytics Project, Not a Career Counselor

Carrier Codex converts raw career data into graphs, comparisons, trends, and honest insights — the kind of information that should exist before you make a decision that shapes the next decade of your life. It's not advice from someone who has it figured out. It's analysis from someone who had to figure it out.
