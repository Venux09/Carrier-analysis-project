The Indian Reality

Only Two Real Choices: PCM or PCB

In over 90% of Indian schools, only two primary combinations are actually offered — Non-Medical (PCM) and Medical (PCB). Compared globally, this is a strikingly narrow menu.

Subject-combination freedom by country

Share of flexibility score — India barely registers
India offers far fewer science subject combinations than the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Germany or Singapore.
More combinations abroad let students build unique skill sets early — India's funnel forces everyone down the same two pipes.
The conclusion isn't despair — it's that self-directed learning has to fill the gap school structure won't.
